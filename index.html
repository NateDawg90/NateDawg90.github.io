Description

A refreshing, empowering, and frothy smoothie that will sweeten your day and buld your muscles!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 banana
  • 1/4 cup pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon high-quality oil (i.e. flax oil)
  • 2 rounded tablespoons Whey protein
  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup water

Directions

Thoroughly blend all ingredients together, adding ice cubes or cold water until the shake reaches the desired consistency. You may make a large enough batch to last you through the day, but make sure to keep it refridgerated and remix as needed before pouring.
