Ingredients

  • a
  • 1/4 banana
  • 1/4 cup pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon high-quality oil (i.e. flax oil)
  • 2 rounded tablespoons Whey protein
  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup water

High Protein Smoothie

Description

A refreshing, empowering, and frothy smoothie that will sweeten your day and buld your muscles!

Directions

  1. Thoroughly blend all ingredients together
  2. Add ice cubes or cold water until the shake reaches the desired consistency
  3. You may make a large enough batch to last you through the day
  4. Make sure to keep it refridgerated and remix as needed before pouring
